Domain Verification

Choose your preferred method to verify domain ownership. This step is required before we can issue your SSL certificate.

HTTP Verification
DNS Verification
Email Verification

HTTP File Verification

Upload a verification file to your web server.

  1. Download the verification file:
    .well-known/pki-validation/verification.txt
  2. Upload the file to your web server at:
    http://your-domain.com/.well-known/pki-validation/verification.txt
  3. Click verify to complete the process

DNS Record Verification

Add a TXT record to your domain's DNS settings.

  1. Add this TXT record to your DNS settings:
    Name: _pki-validation Value: apnassl-verification=randomstring123
  2. Wait for DNS propagation (usually 5-30 minutes)
  3. Click verify to complete the process

Email Verification

Receive a verification email at your domain.

  1. Choose an email address:
    • admin@your-domain.com
    • administrator@your-domain.com
    • webmaster@your-domain.com
    • hostmaster@your-domain.com
  2. Click the verification link in the email
  3. Complete the verification process
